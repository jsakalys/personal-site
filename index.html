About Me

I am a Full Stack Developer aspiring toward a junior role in UI Development & Engineering. I thrive in fast-paced, high stakes environments where I can tackle complex front & back-end projects using the latest frameworks/libraries alongside a motivated, energized team. An adaptable and quick learner, I am stimulated by the immediate challenges faced when implementing cutting-edge, fluid web technologies for optimum speed and scalability.

I'm a huge proponent of open source and the democratization of technology, and have an innate desire to support creative individuals and other developers in their creative/technical pursuits. I'm particularly passionate about Cross-platform App Development and UI Engineering. Although I'm known for my eclectic taste, my design aesthetic is minimal and functional. In my spare time, I enjoy working on pet projects & developing custom skins for native apps!
